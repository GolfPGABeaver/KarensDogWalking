About Karen's Dog Walking

Karen's Dog Walking was set up in Thornton-Cleveleys (Lancashire) in 2019 by partners Karen Lancaster and Charlie Beavers.

After a career change from Karen as a nursery nures and Charlie as a golf coach, they wanted to make a difference for dogs in the local area.

We understand that some dogs have unlimited energy and even some need a person to pop by for a few minutes to ease there seperation anixety.
